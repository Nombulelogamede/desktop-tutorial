Type of hair

So there's three types of porositys that determine your hair types and in my opinoin porosity is one of the most important factors that can influence how one can have healthy hair hence ,for now , i am not going to go into types of curls or kinks eg.4c,3b etc hair.Porosity is your hairs ability to absorb and retain moisture. If you have low porosity your hair doesnt absorb product easily. Medium porosity hair doesnt take long to dry, it takes colour well etc. High porosity hair tends to break easily and fizzy or dry.
